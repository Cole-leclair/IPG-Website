About Us

A small agency, built to stay small on purpose.

Insurance Partner Group is an independent agency based in the Dallas, Texas area — built around the idea that insurance works better with a real point of contact rather than a random call center.

Our Approach

Independent by design.

Being independent means we're not employed by an insurance company — we work for our clients. That changes the incentive: instead of being tied to a single company's prices, we compare terms and pricing across the carriers we represent and bring back the option that actually fits your risk.

It also means the relationship doesn't end once a policy is bound. The same small team that quotes your coverage handles your certificates, your endorsements, your renewals, and your claims — so you're never starting over with someone new.
