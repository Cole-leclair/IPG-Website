    Personal Lines FAQ

    Questions we hear often.

    What's the difference between an HO-3 and an HO-6 policy?

    An HO-3 is a standard homeowners policy for a house you own. An HO-6 covers a condo unit, since the building itself is typically insured separately by the HOA. We'll help you figure out which applies to you.

    Can I bundle home and auto?

    In most cases, yes — bundling often qualifies for a discount and means one renewal date and one point of contact instead of two.

    Do you insure boats, motorcycles, or collector cars?

    Yes, as specialty personal lines alongside your regular auto and homeowners coverage.
